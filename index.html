Scan a cup. Win instantly.

A unique QR code on every cup. The customer scans, enters their email, and plays a scratch-and-win. The prize drops straight into Apple/Google Wallet — no app to install.

  • 🎟️ Every scan wins — rare jackpots, a small consolation otherwise.
  • 📲 Frictionless — wallet pass, not a clunky loyalty app.
  • 📈 Builds a marketing list — every play captures an email.
